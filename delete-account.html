Delete Account

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Swift Logger lets users permanently delete their account and associated data from inside the app.

How to delete your account in the app

  1. Open Swift Logger and sign in to your account.
  2. Go to Profile.
  3. Open Security and password.
  4. Tap Delete My Account.
  5. Confirm the deletion prompt.

What gets deleted

  • Your account profile and authentication record.
  • Your fitness logs, including meals, workouts, water, sleep, supplements, cardio, steps, and weight entries.
  • Your saved settings, targets, split configuration, and AI preferences stored with your account.
  • Your uploaded progress photos and other account-linked app data stored by us.

What may be retained

  • Temporary backup copies and security logs may remain for a limited period if required for fraud prevention, legal compliance, or disaster recovery.
  • Anonymous or aggregated analytics that no longer identify you personally may be retained.

Retention timing

We aim to delete user account data promptly after the deletion request is completed in the app. Some backup or security records may persist for a limited retention period before automatic removal.

Need help?

If you cannot access the app and need help requesting deletion, contact us through the support details provided in our privacy policy.
